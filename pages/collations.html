Collation Options

There are several ‘standard’ collation systems:

  • Flooded infeed round containers, two or three lanes.
  • Lane divider round or shaped containers, one to four lanes.
  • Single row infeed round or shaped containers, multiple lanes.
  • Multi-tier, single row cartons or trays.
  • Multi-tier, high speed cartons or trays.

If none of these are quite right for your application, contact us. Our specialty is custom infeeds for difficult products.
