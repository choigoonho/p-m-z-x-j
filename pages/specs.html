Features

  • Case-over-product packing allows tighter case fits - prevents product damage.
  • All mechanical case erecting (no pins or vacuum cups).
  • Large case magazine - holds 300 B-flute cases.
  • Hot melt or pressure sensitive tape closure.
  • Stainless-steel frame and covers; all aluminum components anodized.
  • All drives inverter controlled.
  • Electrically interlocked safety guards to CE standards.
  • Moisture- and dust-tight enclosures.
  • Interfacing with upstream and downstream equipment.
  • Comprehensive operation and service manual.
  • All parts number stamped for easy replacement.

Cases

Length: 8 - 19” (200 - 480mm)

Width: 5 - 13” (125 - 330mm)

Height: 5.5 - 13” (140 - 330mm)

Cases must be RSC (regular slotted container) corrugated. Standard tolerances are generally satisfactory. Cases may be printer slotter or die cut; A, B, or C flute; single or double wall.

Standard Components

  • Pneumatic valves: SMC or Mac
  • Pneumatic cylinders: SMC
  • P.L.C.: Siemens S7 or Mitsubishi FX
  • Message display: Siemens TD200 or Mitsubishi E100
  • AC drives: S.E.W. - Eurodrive
  • Linear Guidance: Bosch, corrosion protected
  • Conveyor chain: Intralox
